Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our WhatsApp Business API application ("Fruviapp") handles information. The App is designed solely for internal business automation and communication purposes.

Our application does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The App operates as an automated messaging service using the WhatsApp Business API.

3. How We Use Information

The App uses the WhatsApp Business API exclusively to:

4. Data Storage

No personal data is stored by our application. All communications are processed through WhatsApp's servers according to their privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • WhatsApp Business API: Governed by Meta's privacy policy
  • n8n: Used for workflow automation

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there are no data security concerns related to our application specifically. All message security is handled by WhatsApp's infrastructure.

7. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. For questions about messages sent through WhatsApp, please refer to WhatsApp's privacy policy.
