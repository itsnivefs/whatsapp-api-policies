Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our WhatsApp Business API application ("Fruviapp") handles information. The App is designed solely for internal business automation and communication purposes.

2. Information We Collect

Our application may collect and process the following information:

  • Contact Information: Phone numbers for message delivery
  • Lead Status Data: Customer lead status and engagement levels
  • Message Content: Messages sent to and received from customers (when AI response features are enabled)
  • Marketing Preferences: Customer preferences for marketing communications

3. How We Use Information

The App uses collected information to:

  • Send automated business notifications and updates
  • Deliver targeted marketing messages based on lead status
  • Process customer inquiries through AI-powered responses (when enabled)
  • Facilitate workflow automation through n8n integration
  • Analyze customer engagement for business improvement

4. Data Storage and Retention

We may temporarily store:

  • Phone numbers and lead status information for messaging automation
  • Message history for AI context (when AI features are enabled)
  • Marketing preferences and engagement data

Data is retained only as long as necessary for business purposes and is securely deleted when no longer needed.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • WhatsApp Business API: Governed by Meta's privacy policy
  • n8n: Used for workflow automation
  • AI/LLM Services: May be used for automated response generation (when enabled)

6. Marketing Communications

We may send marketing messages based on your lead status and preferences. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any marketing message
  • Contacting us directly using the information below

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
